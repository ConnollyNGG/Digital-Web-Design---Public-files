Grand Canyon Vacation

We went to the Grand Canyon this summer while we were in Arizona. We thought you would like to see some of our awesome pictures. We took so many shots, but these were really the best.

  • Looking down Through the rocks

You can see how beautiful the Grand Canyon can be with this picture. We were able to stand right on the edge of the viewing path and shoot this scene. This is the image that I use on my desktop. It is a constant reminder to me about how wonderful that vacation was.

We stayed in the Grand Canyon lodge that trip, which is very close to the visitor's center. The center is just great. They conduct many classes there and provide you with wonderful information about how the canyon was formed.
